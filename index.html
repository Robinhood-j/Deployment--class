About Me

Get to know the photographer behind the lens

Hello, I'm Jacinta Wanjiku

With over 10 years of professional photography experience, I specialize in capturing authentic moments and emotions through my lens.

My journey began when I received my first camera as a birthday gift at age 16. Since then, I've developed my skills through formal education and countless hours behind the camera.

My approach is to create a comfortable, enjoyable experience that results in natural, beautiful images you'll treasure for years to come.

When I'm not photographing clients, you can find me exploring nature trails with my camera, teaching photography workshops, or spending time with my family.
